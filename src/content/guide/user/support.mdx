## Description

The Support panel provides quick access to contact options for different kinds of issues, suggestions, and account-related assistance.

---

## Buttons

1. Switch to the **Account** panel.
2. Switch to the **Support** panel.
3. Send an email for general support.
4. Send an email to report a bug.
5. Send an email to suggest a new feature.
6. Send an email to suggest an improvement.
7. Send an email for account-related issues.
8. Send an email for subscription-related questions.
