## Description

The Subscription panel displays the user&apos;s current plan and billing details. It also provides options to cancel the subscription or navigate to other account-related panels.

## Buttons

1. Switch to the **Subscription** panel.
2. Switch to the **Password** panel.
3. Switch to the **Support** panel.
4. Cancel the current subscription.

## Labels

1. A. Current plan (Monthly plan or Yearly plan).
2. B. Current plan price ($ / paying duration).
3. C. Next billing date.
