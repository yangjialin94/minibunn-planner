## Description

The Support panel provides quick access to contact options for different kinds of issues, suggestions, and account-related assistance.

---

## Buttons

1. Switch to the **Subscription** panel.
2. Switch to the **Password** panel.
3. Switch to the **Support** panel.
4. Send an email for general support.
5. Send an email to report a bug.
6. Send an email to suggest a new feature.
7. Send an email to suggest an improvement.
8. Send an email for account-related issues.
9. Send an email for subscription-related questions.
