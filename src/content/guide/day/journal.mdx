## Description

The journal panel allows users to reflect and document their thoughts for the day. It includes a subject and entry field, and is tied to the selected calendar date.

## Buttons

1. Switch to the **Tasks** panel.
2. Switch to the **Journal** panel.
3. Go to the previous day.
4. Go to the next day.
5. Clear the journal&apos;s subject and entry.

## Inputs

1. I. Journal subject input field.
2. II. Journal entry input field.

## Labels

1. A. Selected date and time.
